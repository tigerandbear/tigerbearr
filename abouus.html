About Us

Learn about our mission, our team, and how we're making a difference for animals.

Our Mission

Giving Animals a Second Chance

At Tiger Bear Express and Rescue, our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and re-home abandoned, neglected, and abused animals. We are dedicated to providing compassionate care and finding loving forever homes for every animal that comes into our care. We believe every animal deserves a second chance at happiness, and we work tirelessly to make that a reality.

Our Vision

A Community Where Every Animal is Loved and Cherished

We envision a community where every animal is treated with kindness, respect, and compassion. Our goal is to create a world where no animal suffers from neglect or abuse and where every pet has a safe, loving home. We strive to be a leading voice for animal welfare and to inspire others to join us in our mission.

Our Values

The Principles That Guide Our Work

Compassion

We approach every animal with kindness, understanding, and empathy, recognizing their inherent worth and need for love.

Community

We believe in the power of community involvement and collaboration to achieve our goals and make a greater impact on animal welfare.

Dedication

We are unwavering in our commitment to providing the highest quality of care for every animal and finding them the perfect forever home.

Our History

A Timeline of Growth and Impact

  • 2018

    Founded with a Passion

    Tiger Bear Express and Rescue was founded by Patsy C. Bartis with a vision to help animals in need.

  • 2020

    Expanding Our Reach

    We expanded our foster network and began partnering with local shelters.

  • 2022

    Reaching Milestones

    We celebrated placing over 500 animals in loving homes.

  • Today

    Continuing Our Mission

    We continue to rescue, rehabilitate, and re-home animals, making a lasting impact on their lives and the community.
