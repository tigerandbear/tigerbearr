About Tiger Bear Express and Rescue

Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and re-home animals in need.

Tiger Bear Express and Rescue is dedicated to providing compassionate care for abandoned and neglected animals. We strive to give each animal a second chance at a happy and healthy life through rescue, rehabilitation, and adoption. We believe every animal deserves love, and we work tirelessly to find the perfect forever home for each one.

Your support is vital to our mission. Whether you donate, volunteer, or adopt, you become part of our community dedicated to making a difference in the lives of animals. Thank you for helping us give them the second chance they deserve!

What We Do

We offer a range of services to support animals and the community.

Animal Rescue

We rescue animals from shelters, streets, and other challenging situations.

Adoption Services

We carefully match rescued animals with loving families and forever homes.

Medical Care

We provide necessary medical care, including vaccinations and spay/neuter services.

Foster Care

Temporary homes provide a nurturing environment while animals await adoption.

Donations

Your generous donations help us provide essential care and services.
